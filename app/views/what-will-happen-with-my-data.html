What will happen with my data?

Your documents will be stored securely until your scheduled appointment.

They can only be accessed by the customer service agent running your appointment.

Once we have confirmed your identity, we will erase any personal ID documents from our system.

We will store a verification certificate on file until your photographic ID expires.

We may have to contact you in the future to re-confirm your details.
