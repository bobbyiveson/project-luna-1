How to upload

Make sure:

  • your background is clear
  • your documents are well lit and in focus
  • all details are visible

When taking a photo:

  • do not use flash as this can blur the image
  • take one photo of each document

To upload a document:

  • select type of photo ID from the dropdown menu
  • choose photo ID file and select upload
  • provide expiration date for chosen document
