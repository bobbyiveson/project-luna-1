How to upload

Make sure your background is clear

Please make sure your documents are well lit, in focus, with all details included and minimal background distractions.

Take photo

Do not use flash as this can blur image. If you want to upload multiple documents, take one photo of each.

Upload document

Select chosen file type from dropdown menu, then upload image. There are some restrictions on file type and file size, but most photos taken with a smartphone or digital camera should be fine. There are more details about these restrictions on the page.
